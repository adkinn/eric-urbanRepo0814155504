Clicks are what you pay for. Clicks typically include a customer clicking an ad on a search results page or on a website on the search network. Clicks can also come from other sources (for example, spiders, robots, and test servers). For more information, see [Bing Ads click measurement: description of methodology](https://advertise.bingads.microsoft.com/resources/policies/bing-ads-click-measurement-description-of-methodology).
